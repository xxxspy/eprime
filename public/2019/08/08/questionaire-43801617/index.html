Title of Research: Evaluation Strategies and Influencing Factors of Social Media Information: Evidence from Weibo
研究题目：社交媒体信息可信度评价策略及影响因素:基于微博

Researchers 研究员
Researchers: Xinlan Chen, xchen126@sheffield.ac.uk
Supervisor: Jonathan Foster, j.j.foster@sheffield.ac.uk

Purpose of the research 研究目的

Aim:
— This paper mainly uses the combination of literature analysis, qualitative analysis and quantitative analysis, interviews and questionnaires. The main research objectives are: to explore the strategies of users’ perception of information credibility using Sina Weibo and its influencing factors, so as to serve users. Provide practical advice for better identification of trusted information

Objectives:
-To carry out a literature review on social media, information credibility research and representative information credibility evaluation strategies
-To design interview and questionnaire to probe relevant cognitive and evaluation strategies
-To carry out a survey of users using social media - Weibo
-To analyse and discuss the evaluation strategies and influencing factors of the credibility of social media information
-To get a complete conclusion and summarize the limitations of the study

目标:
-本文主要采用文献分析、定性分析与定量分析相结合、访谈与问卷调查相结合的方法。主要研究目的是:探讨用户使用新浪微博感知信息可信度的策略及其影响因素，为用户服务。为更好地识别可信信息提供实用建议

目的:
-对社交媒体、信息可信度研究和代表性信息可信度评价策略进行文献综述
-设计访谈和问卷，探讨相关认知和评价策略
-对使用社交媒体——微博的用户进行调查
-分析探讨社交媒体信息可信度的评价策略及影响因素
-得出一个完整的结论，并总结研究的局限性

Who will be participating? 谁将参加?
Potential Participants will be Chinese people whose age is over 18 years old and use the social media–Weibo.
潜在的参与者将是年龄在18岁以上、使用社交媒体——微博的中国人。

What will you be asked to do? 你会被要求做什么?
Researchers will ask you to complete a short questionnaire about people using assessment strategies to make information credibility judgments (your age, gender, etc.) so that we have a profile of the group of participants.
Researchers will ask :gender, education level, information assessment awareness, reading preference and reading intensity and the motivation and frequency of the evaluation of the credibility of social media information.

研究人员将要求你完成一份简短的问卷，关于人们使用评估策略来做出信息可信度判断(你的年龄、性别等)，这样我们就有了一组参与者的简介。
研究人员将会问:性别、受教育程度、信息评估意识、阅读偏好和阅读强度以及评价社交媒体信息可信度的动机和频率。

The risks of participating are the same as those experienced in everyday life.
参与的风险与日常生活中的风险相同。

What data will I collect? 我将收集什么数据?
The questions are usually multiple choice questions. Researchers will collect: gender, education level, information assessment awareness, reading preference and reading intensity and the factors of the evaluation of the credibility of social media information.
这些问题通常是多项选择题。研究人员会收集:性别、受教育程度、信息评价意识、阅读偏好和阅读强度以及评价社交媒体信息可信度的影响因素。

What will I do with the data? 我将如何处理这些数据?
Participants will not be asked to provide any personal identifying information about themselves. Data which could uniquely identify someone will not be gathered as part of the questionnaire data for analysis.
If participants (and potential participants) contact the researchers directly with questions about the study, it is possible that the researchers will come into contact with personal data such as the participant’s name and/or email address. As this information is neither necessary or useful for the current study, if and when it is provided the researchers will delete it.
Researchers will use the “Questionnaire Star” online survey tool to create characters and collect data. The questionnaire tool can guarantee the anonymity of respondents when they use it.
The data will be stored on the iSchool’s secure research server, it is also stored on the researcher’s personal computer, and is only used by researchers themselves and is password protected. When the study is complete, no further analysis is required and the data will be deleted.

参加者不会被要求提供任何有关自己的个人身份资料。能够唯一识别某人的数据将不作为问卷数据的一部分进行分析。
如果参与者(和潜在的参与者)直接与研究人员联系有关研究的问题，研究人员可能会接触到参与者的姓名和/或电子邮件地址等个人数据。由于这些信息对当前的研究既不是必要的也不是有用的，如果提供了这些信息，研究人员将删除它。
研究人员将使用“问卷星”在线调查工具来创建字符和收集数据。问卷工具可以保证被调查者在使用时的匿名性。
这些数据将存储在isschools的安全研究服务器上，也存储在研究人员的个人电脑上，只供研究人员自己使用，并有密码保护。当研究完成时，不需要进一步的分析，数据将被删除。

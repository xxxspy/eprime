问卷介绍-Research on people trust on online travel influencers/bloggers 对网上旅游影响者／博主的信任研究

问卷指导语

我们正在针对网络时代下有影响力的人的被信任现状进行调查，以旅游博主为研究对象，找到他们被信任的前因和后果，构建合理的信任模型。为了研究的真实性、严谨性，特拟定有关问题向您展开调查。问题回答没有对错之分，请您根据实际情况和真实想法选择合适的选项。您的回答仅供研究使用，完全匿名，不作任何商业用途，不会对您个人产生任何不良影响，请放心填答。再次感谢您的支持和配合。

Researchers
Shunyan Liang (University of Sheffield)
Purpose of the research
The research explores why people trust online influencers. It will identify how trust created and the factors affecting trust between followers and influencers.
Who will be participating?
We are inviting adults over the age of 18 using social media, and are residents of the UK and China.
What will you be asked to do?
We will ask you to answer series of questions about your basic information (gender and ages) and trust behaviours toward online influencers. You will complete this task via the Wenjuanxing platform/Google Forms. This task will take about 8 minutes.
The content of the survey will be mainly about the following topics:

  1. Your experiences of trusting influencers online at the beginning
  2. Your opinion of the factors affecting you to trust influencers (Score them)
    What are the potential risks of participating?
    The risks of participating are the same as those experienced in everyday life. It is not expected that the questions will return any offensive content, however whilst participants are free to stop the survey.
    What data will I collect?
    We will collect your responses to the task questions. We will not ask you to provide any identifying information, and when we download the data from Wenjuanxing platform/Google Forms, we will delete your user name, so the responses to the tasks will then be anonymous.
    What will I do with the data?
    All digital data collected will be stored in the School’s Research Data drive. If transferring potentially identifiable data between researchers on the survey, we will ensure that data is encrypted/password protected. The data will be used only for the research. The data will have stored on the Information School’s research data drive which can be accessed only by me, my supervisor, and the School’s Examinations Officer and ICT staff operating the facility. The data will be destroyed once my degree has been awarded.
    Will your participation be confidential?
    When we download your responses from Wenjuanxing platform/Google Forms, we will delete your user name, so your responses will not be linked back to you. I will anonymize the data and code the computer files with a random number prior to analysis. No other potentially identifying information will be collected as part of this task.
    What will happen to the results of the research project?
    The results of this study will be included in my master’s dissertation which will be publicly available. Please contact the School in six months, and will inform the development of future research projects.
    What is the legal basis for processing your personal data?
    The University of Sheffield will act as the Data Controller for this study. This means that the University is responsible for looking after your information and using it properly. In order to collect and use your personal information as part of this research project, we must have a basis in law to do so. The basis that we are using is that the research is ‘a task in the public interest’.
    Declaration of consent
    •I confirm that I have read and understand the description of the research project, and that I have had an opportunity to ask questions about the project.
    •I understand that my participation is voluntary and that I am free to withdraw at any time without any negative consequences.
    •I understand that if I withdraw I can request for the data I have already provided to be deleted, however this might not be possible if the data has already been anonymised or findings published.
    •I understand that I may decline to answer any particular question or questions, or to do any of the activities.
    •I understand that my responses will be kept strictly confidential, that my name or identity will not be linked to any research materials, and that I will not be identified or identifiable in any report or reports that result from the research, unless I have agreed otherwise.
    •I give permission for all the research team members to have access to my responses.
    •I give permission for the research team to re-use my data for future research as specified above.
    •I agree to take part in the research project as described above.

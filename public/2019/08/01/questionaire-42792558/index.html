Determinants of eWOM’s perceived credibility in Chinese colour cosmetic market-问卷计分方式

问卷指导语

Dear ladies and gentlemen,
I am a master student at the University of Bristol, and you are invited to participate in the survey of my dissertation. The purpose of the questionnaire is to explore the determinants of eWOM’s perceived credibility and how they affect eWOM adoption in the Chinese colour cosmetic market.
女士们先生们，我是布里斯托大学的研究生，现在邀请您参与我的毕业论文问卷调查。这份问卷的目的是探讨在中国彩色化妆品市场中，eWOM（电子口碑）感知可信度的决定因素，以及这些因素如何影响用户对eWOM的采用。

This questionnaire will take you about three minutes, and your participation is voluntarily and free to withdraw at any time without consequences. All the questionnaires will be anonymous and will be only used for academic research purpose. In addition, all the collected data will be stored on University Drive and destroyed immediately after the research completed.
完成本问卷大约需要三分钟时间，您的参与是自愿的并且可以随时无理由退出。所有问卷都是匿名的，仅用于学术研究目的。此外，所有收集到的数据将存储于University Drive，并在研究完成后立即销毁。

Definitions’ interpretation（定义解释）:

  1. Electronic word-of-mouth (eWOM) is generally defined as positive and negative descriptions of products and services that potential, actual or former consumers share with other consumers through online platforms (Hennig-Thurau, et al., 2004). 电子口碑(eWOM)一般定义为对潜在、实际或以前消费者通过在线平台分享给其他消费者的关于产品和服务的正面或负面描述。
  2. Colour cosmetics are combinations of chemicals for the purpose of enhancing the appearance or imparting aroma, such as with facial make-up products, nail care products, lip care products and eye make-up products (Roy, 2017). 彩色化妆品是改善外观或传递香气的化学品组合，如面部化妆产品、指甲护理产品、唇部护理产品和眼妆产品。

该问卷主要用于统计学、教育学、计量经济学、心理学、行为经济学专业的样本收集。

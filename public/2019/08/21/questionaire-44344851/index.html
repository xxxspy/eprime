问卷介绍-Chinese Young Women's Consuming Behavior toward Purchasing Luxury Fashion Goods

问卷指导语

The aim of this study is to find out the motives that are behind Chinese women consumers purchasing luxury fashion branded products. Information provided will be used for academic purposes only. Please note that there are no good or bad responses.

This study is mainly aimed at young women aged 22-30. If you meet the requirements and have luxury shopping experience, you are welcome to fill in the questionnaire. Your participation in this research study is voluntary. You may choose not to participate. If you decide to participate in this research survey, you may withdraw at any time. If you decide not to participate in this study or if you withdraw from participating at any time, you will not be penalized.

The procedure involves filling an online survey that will take approximately 5-10 minutes. Your responses will be confidential and we do not collect identifying information such as your name, email address or IP address.

We will do our best to keep your information confidential. All data is stored in a password protected electronic format. To help protect your confidentiality, the surveys will not contain information that will personally identify you.

该问卷主要用于计量经济学、行为经济学、心理学、教育学、统计学专业的样本收集。

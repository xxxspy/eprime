问卷指导语

You are being invited to participate in a research study of brands and how they can be extended to cover other uses or products such as Xiaomi. This study is being done by Chen Chunyu from the University of Leeds as part of my Master’s degree.
Your participation is completely voluntary, and you can opt-out at any time. I will ensure that your participation is kept confidential and that only anonymous information will be used for academic purposes. If you have any questions, please contact me at ml17c9c@leeds.ac.uk. Thanks for your help! Further information is available via the University of Leeds Privacy Notice.
I would be very grateful if you could help me with this study by completing the questionnaire.

您的参与完全是自愿的，您可以随时退出。我将确保您的参与是保密的，只有匿名信息将用于学术目的。如果您有任何问题，请通过ml17c9c@leeds.ac.uk与我联系。谢谢你的帮助!更多信息可通过利兹大学隐私公告获得。

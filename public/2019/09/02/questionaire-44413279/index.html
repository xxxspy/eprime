Employee Motivation and Job Satisfaction(问卷内容)

问卷指导语

I am a student pursuing MSc Management, University of Birmingham and working on a dissertation to fulfil the academic requirement of a Master’s degree. The purpose of this study is to assess relationship between employee motivation and job satisfaction among Chinese SME hotels with a focus on the 7 Days Inn. Please take a few minutes of your time to complete this survey. Your honest opinion will be totally anonymous, but your views, combined with those of participating members are extremely vital in this study. This survey will take just around 15 minutes of your time. All the information collected from the survey will be strictly confidential and will only be used for academic purpose. Your participation is highly appreciated in this study.

该问卷主要用于统计学、教育学、心理学、计量经济学、行为经济学专业的样本收集。

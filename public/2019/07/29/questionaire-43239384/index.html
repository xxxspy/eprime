问卷指导语

Hello, I’m a student at the University of Nottingham. I am conducting a survey for my dissertation. This survey is on Tik Tok & Kwai users’ attitude towards short video social media advertising in China. This survey will take you 3-5 mins.

You will be asked to fill out the questionnaire about 1) Your use of short video social media, 2) The influence of your attitude toward advertisements on your purchase intention, 3) The influence of the subjective norms on your purchase intention, 4) The influence of perceived behavior on your purchase intention, 5) General questions about yourself.

All the questions are based on the short video social media. Please tick the option that best express your actual situation. Thank you for your time.

The researcher assures you that your participation in this study is entirely voluntary. Please feel free if you wish to withdraw from this survey at any time. If you choose to withdraw, the data you provided will be destroyed and will not be used for any analysis.

This study is anonymous, this meaning that the collected data cannot be linked with individuals. You are not required to provide your name or any personal contact information. The researcher assures you that your data will be kept strictly confidential. The research is being undertaken in line with the University of Nottingham Research Ethics Guidelines.

If you have any questions about this study, or if you would wish to receive the report of the study result, please do not hesitate to contact the researcher.
Contact details:
Tiannan Gao
Email: lixtg14@nottingham.ac.uk

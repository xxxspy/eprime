问卷介绍-Research “The influence of technology on jewellery market”

问卷指导语

Thank you for kindly participating in this questionnaire. Your opinions are very important to me, and may be used to support my research on the development of science and technology jewellery and the investigation of how science and technology jewellery becomes a luxury brand of jewellery for the MA Luxury Jewellery Management.

Please bear in mind that your participation is voluntary, and you are free to leave the questionnaire at any moment.

Science and technology jewellery are new type of jewellery design today. It combines traditional jewellery and technology. It can play the role of decoration and people’s life at the same time. For example, we can use the ring to open the door and lock, change the way of wearing jewellery (necklace into bracelet, ring into necklace), and use jewellery to monitor human health. The jewellery with science and technology needs some development before it can be accepted by the market. Questionnaires about your technology jewellery will be studied.

（*Required）

Available at: Maclin, E. (2018). The Effects of Modern Technology on the Jewelry Industry - Thrive Global. [online] Thriveglobal.com. Available at: https://thriveglobal.com/stories/the-effects-of-modern-technology-on-the-jewelry-industry/ [Accessed 16 Jun. 2019].

该问卷主要用于行为经济学、心理学、教育学、计量经济学、统计学专业的样本收集。

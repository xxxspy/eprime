Disclaimer: The primary purpose of this questionnaire is to collect data for a research project that is designed to explore the influence of luxury brand social media marketing on young consumers’ purchasing behaviour in China. Specifically, the project aims to understand the dynamics and complexity of Generation Z female consumers’ purchasing behaviour and decision process, so that luxury fashion brands can use this result to improve their marketing strategies and then increase sales and popularity. Below are a few questions that should take you less than 10 minutes. If you have any further inquiry, please email: ll1a18@soton.ac.uk.

免责声明：本调查问卷的主要目的是收集研究项目的数据，旨在探讨奢侈品牌社交媒体营销对中国年轻消费者购买行为的影响。 具体而言，该项目旨在了解Z时代女性消费者购买行为和决策过程的动态性和复杂性，以便奢侈时尚品牌可以利用这一结果来改善其营销策略，从而提高销售和人气。 以下是一些占用您不到10分钟的问题。 如果您有任何进一步的询问，请发送电子邮件至：ll1a18@soton.ac.uk。

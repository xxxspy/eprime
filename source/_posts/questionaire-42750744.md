### 问卷指导语

该问卷收集的数据为本人研究生论文研究所用。论文研究方向为在线旅游论坛。问卷中的提到的在线旅游论坛是指如马蜂窝，穷游等。问卷中提到的旅游产品或者服务是指机票，酒店，交通门票或者当地团等。
This survey collects data is for my Master's Thesis. The research tries to investigate the online travel communities. An online travel community can refer to the one that you are familiar with such as Qyer.com or Mafengwo.com and etc. A tourism product or services can refer to flights, hotels, traffic and attraction tickets, local tours and etc.

所有收集的数据会严格的保密和只用于本人研究生论文的研究。问卷是匿名的，无法辨识参与者。
All the data is strictly confidential and only used for non-commercial academic research purposes. The survey is anonymous: it is impossible to recognize participants.

本问卷一共有42道问题。可能将需要大概20分钟的时间来完成。烦请务必完成所有的问题。将会有两名随机的参与者得到免费的电影票作为参与的奖励。
There are 42 questions in total. It will take 20 mins to finish all the survey questions. Please answer all the questions. There will be two random respondents who will receive movie tickets for participant for the survey.

该问卷主要用于行为经济学、心理学、教育学、计量经济学、统计学专业的样本收集。

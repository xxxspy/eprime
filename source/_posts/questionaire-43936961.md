你好，我是来自谢菲尔德大学管理学院创意文化产业管理的一名硕士研究生。我们诚邀您参加一项名为“快时尚品牌的营销策略对大学生购买行为的影响”的在线调查。这项调查是我在谢菲尔德大学硕士毕业论文中的一部分，希望您可以帮助我完成这项调查。
在您决定之前，请花时间仔细阅读以下信息，并决定您是否愿意参加。感谢您阅读本文。
您是快时尚品牌的目标市场之一，也是本次研究的目标群体。您的意见将是很有价值的参考。约150名其他参与者将在网上加入。如果您决定参加，你将得到这张信息表(并被要求签署一份同意书)，您仍然有权力在任何时候退出
您将在网上填写一份问卷，可能需要10分钟。我将会问您购买快时尚产品的频率，以及您对快时尚品牌营销策略的看法。您需要根据您的个人经验和观点来回答问题。您可以拒绝任何您不想回答的问题。此外，请不要将问卷中填写的个人内容转发给他人，以确保您的个人信息安全。我们在研究过程中收集到的关于您的所有信息都将严格保密。您不会在任何报告或出版物中被识别。您的机构将不会被识别。这些匿名数据不允许任何个人或他们的组织识别。
如需进一步了解，请联络本项目主管Roger Bennett教授(Roger Bennett@sheffield.ac.uk)和谢菲尔德大学管理学院学生李丽莎(Lli70@sheffield.ac.uk)。


Hello, I am a master's student from the University of Sheffield who major in Creative and Culture Industry Management. We invite you to participate in an online survey called "The influence of fast fashion brand marketing strategies on university students' purchasing behavior." This survey is part of my master's dissertation at the University of Sheffield and I hope that you can help me with this survey.
Before you decide, please take the time to read the following information carefully and decide if you are willing to participate. Thank you for reading this article.
You are one of the target markets for fast fashion brands and the target group for this dissertation. Your opinion will be a valuable reference. About 150 other participants will join the questionnaire . If you decide to participate, you will get this information form (and be asked to sign a consent form), you can still opt out at any time.
You will fill out a questionnaire online and it may take up to 10 minutes. I will ask you how often you buy a fast fashion product and what you think about the fast fashion brand marketing strategy. You need to answer questions based on your personal experience and opinions. You can reject any questions you don't want to answer. In addition, please do not forward the personal content filled in the questionnaire to others to ensure the security of your personal information.
All information we collect about you during the dissertation process will be kept strictly confidential. You will not be identified in any reports or publications. Your institution will not be identified. These anonymous data do not allow identification by any individual or their organization.
I will ask you how often you buy a fast fashion product and what you think about the fast fashion brand marketing strategy.
For further information, please contact the supervisor of this Master dissertation Professor Roger Bennett (Roger Bennett@sheffield.ac.uk).
And student Li Lisha, University of Sheffield School of Management, (Lli70@sheffield.ac.uk).

### 问卷指导语

尊敬的先生/女士
我是谢菲尔德大学管理学院的一名学生。我正在进行一项关于消费者对社交媒体上与品牌相关的病毒式营销内容分享行为的调查，这项调查是针对新浪微博用户的实证研究。非常感谢您对问卷的回答，这对我的研究非常重要。
答案没有对错之分。您有权在调查期间随时退出。问卷的提交说明你理解并愿意参与这项研究。本问卷所提供的资料将严格保密，只作研究用途。个人信息不会被披露。
Dear sir/ madam,
I am a student from the School of Management in University of Sheffield. I am conducting a survey about consumers’ sharing behavior towards brand-related viral marketing contents on social media and this survey is an empirical study for Sina Weibo’s users. Your responses to the questionnaire are most appreciated and important to my research.
There are no right or wrong answers. You have the right to withdraw at any time during the survey. The submission of the questionnaire indicates that you understand and are willing to participate in the study.All information conducted in this questionnaire will be kept strictly confidential and used only for research purpose. No individual information will be disclosed.

该问卷主要用于行为经济学、教育学、统计学、心理学、计量经济学专业的样本收集。

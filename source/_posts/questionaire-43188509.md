### 问卷指导语

The research is being carried out as a part of Master’s program at University of Southampton. Purpose of the study is to analyse and understand if the young consumers in China tend to purchase from specific fashion labels and brands because of their endorsement by celebrities or not. You are a young consumer in China who buys from fashion brands and hence your perspective and responses are expected to be helpful in being able to achieve the objectives of the study. You are expected to fill in the questionnaire, which would take around 10-15 minutes of your time. Remain assured that the questionnaire asks for no personal data and information and the data that is collected from you would be used only for academic purposes and not shared by a third party. It is totally your choice and willingness to be able to participate in the study and you can even withdraw from the study before the 15th of August before the report is prepared. By filling in the questionnaire, you are giving your consent to participate in the study such that the data that you give can be utilised for carrying out the study and defining the end objectives.

该问卷主要用于心理学、统计学、行为经济学、教育学、计量经济学专业的样本收集。

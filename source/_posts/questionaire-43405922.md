### 问卷指导语

Dear Sir/Madam,

Thank you for taking time to complete this questionnaire. The primary purpose of this questionnaire is to collect data for a research project  designed to explore factors that influence consumers' purchasing behaviour. It would take you less than 5 minutes to complete.
Disclaimer: This is completely anonymous, and the data will be maintained and not shared in any way without your individual consent. Data obtained in the research will be used for academic purpose alone and not for any other external use.
Thank you very mufor your participation!

十分感谢您的参与!

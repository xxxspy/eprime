### 问卷指导语

Welcome and thank you for considering taking part in this online survey. The purpose of this study is to gain information relating to the determinants of eSports consumption intention. You will be asked to complete an anonymous online survey, which should take no longer than 5 minutes to complete. You do not need to do anything before completing the survey. This is a low risk activity and no disadvantages or risks have been identified in association with participating. You must be over the age of 18 and have the capacity to fully understand and consent to this research. You voluntarily agree to take part in this study.

该问卷主要用于教育学、心理学、计量经济学、行为经济学、统计学专业的样本收集。

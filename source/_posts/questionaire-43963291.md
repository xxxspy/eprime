### 问卷指导语

This is a survey on the influence digitalisation has had on the TV, Film and Video industry
这个问卷是关于数字化转型对视频还有电影行业的影响
The TV, Film and Video industry witnessed an evolution under the emergence of digitalisation. No doubt, this has led the development of new technologies in which had contributed to numerous new opportunities within the industry. Creators are said to be granted increased capacity, flexibility and support in producing their work, and customers are said to be witnessing increase in content quality and choices. However, research has also identified a variety of uncertainties that are needed to be clarified in order to further building onto the current knowledge of the influence digitalisation has on the TV, Film and Video Industry.
最近电子技术使这个行业见证了一个很大变化。新的科技和技术在这个行业创造了很多商机， 创作者拥有了不少新的辅佐软件跟机器来到制作视频跟电影。观众也见证了视频多元化，现有的选择比以前增加了。然而，不少的文献提到一系列的未知之素关于电子化对这个行业的影响。这些未知之素需要跟多的研究来到解答。
Are you a video content consumer/ creator? If the answer is yes, you are invited amongst many others to participate in this survey!
您是视频消费者/创作者吗？如果你是，那么您将被邀请参加本次调查！
Participation is voluntary
参与是自愿的
You can withdraw at any time during the survey and you can also withdraw your data at any time
您可以在调查期间随时撤回，也可以随时撤回您的数据
Completion of this survey will take approximately 5 - 8 mins consisting of 27 questions about you and your opinions / views on various phenomenon occurring within the industry. You will not be asked for your name or anything specific items that are able to identify you
The findings from this survey will be used for my post graduate level dissertation
完成本调查大约需要5-8分钟，其中包括27个关于您的问题以及您对行业内各种现象的看法/观点。系统不会要求您提供您的姓名或能够识别您身份的任何特定物品
本次调查的结果将用于我的研究生论文
All data gathered from this survey will be kept confidential, both during and after the study. All data will be destroyed once completion of study
从研究中收集的所有数据将在研究期间和之后保密。一旦完成研究，所有数据将被销毁
This study has been reviewed by my supervisor Amit Mitra
本研究已由我的主管Amit Mitra审核
If you have any questions, you are welcomed to contact me at : yl18581@bristol.ac.uk
如果您有任何疑问，欢迎您与我联系：yl18581@bristol.ac.uk
If you have any concerns related to your participation in this study please direct them to the Faculty of Social Science Research Ethics Committee, via Liam McKervey, Research Governance and Ethics Officer (Tel: 0117 331 7472, email: Liam.McKervey@bristol.ac.uk)
如果您对参与本研究有任何疑虑，请通过研究治理和道德官Liam McKervey将其指导给社会科学研究伦理委员会（电话：0117 331 7472，电子邮件：Liam.McKervey@bristol.ac .UK）
Thank you for your participation !
感谢您的参与 ！

### 问卷指导语

欢迎您参与调查！

本问卷是我毕业论文的一部分，不涉及任何商业用途。

这份问卷旨在了解Beacon在零售业的运用以及对顾客的影响。Beacon是可以理解为微小的传感器，在零售业的用途，一般是用来连接您的智能手机，在您到访一家店铺时，向您推送相关的促销优惠信息。它作用于低功耗的蓝牙技术，也被认为是物联网在新零售领域的主要应用之一。更多关于Beacon的信息，可以观看视频了解：https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZGL0HpNm5BY。

这份调查共有34题，预计花费5分钟完成。
如有任何疑问欢迎联系我：m.hu-18@student.lboro.ac.uk.

Thanks for your participation!
This research is conducted as part of my dissertation, which is non-commercial in nature.

This survey seeks to understand the effect of Beacons in retail and how they can benefit customers. Beacons refer to small sensors, which communicate with smartphones to show you instant promotions and locational information within retail stores. The work on low energy Bluetooth technology and are touted to become a major area of implementation of Internet of Things, especially in the retail industry. For more information on this please watch this three minute video on Beacons in retail. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZGL0HpNm5BY

The survey has 34 questions and it is expected to consume 5 minutes to finish.
If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me: m.hu-18@student.lboro.ac.uk

该问卷主要用于心理学、计量经济学、教育学、行为经济学、统计学专业的样本收集。

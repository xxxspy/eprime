### 问卷指导语

This is a questionnaire for testing the impact of credibility and para-social interaction of social media influencers on purchase intention in the Chinese beauty market.  The investigator will keep participant's information in a safe place and all questionnaires will remain completely anonymous. Information will not disclose or forward it to a third party without consent of participant. Personal data will be stored securely during the study and will be treated properly after the study.
这是一份研究中国市场中社交媒体影响者的社会互动和可信度对消费者购买意向影响的调查问卷。研究者将参与者的信息保存在安全的地方，所有问卷将保持完全匿名。未经参与者同意，研究者不会将其泄露或转发给第三方。个人资料将于研究期间妥善储存，并于研究后妥善处理。

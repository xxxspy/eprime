### 问卷指导语

This is a questionnaire about understanding the impact of credibility of celebrity endorsements on brand equity in the fast fashion industry. In today's fast fashion industry, it is very common for brands to use celebrity endorsements to increase their reputation and profit. At the same time, the credibility of celebrities is an important factor affecting the effect of endorsement. Various manufacturers spend a lot of energy and money to choose celebrity endorsements to attract the attention of young consumers in the shortest possible time. But whether this impact has a positive impact on brand equity as expected is the focus of this survey. Your completion will be an important reference for this research topic. In order to respect your privacy, this survey questionnaire is filled in anonymously. The survey data is for research and reference purposes only, and will not leak. Thank you for your support.

该问卷主要用于心理学、教育学、统计学、行为经济学、计量经济学专业的样本收集。

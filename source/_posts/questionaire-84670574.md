### 问卷指导语

您好，
我们邀请您参加一项关于电视广告中女性形象的调查。请根据您平时所观看广告的真实想法回答以下问题。研究过程中所有关于您的信息都将被严格保密。所有的数据仅用于本次研究的调查和统计。十分感谢您的合作与支持。
如果有任何问题，请联系一下地址：
jl751@student.le.ac.uk

Hello,
We invite you to participate in a study of women's portrayal in TV advertising. Please answer the following questions based on your thought of TV commercial which you see. All the information about you during the study will be kept strictlly confidential. All the data will be used for survey and statistical purpose only for this study.
Thank you for the support  and cooperation.
If you have any questions, please contact the following address: jl751@student.le.ac.uk

该问卷主要用于教育学、心理学、行为经济学、计量经济学、统计学专业的样本收集。

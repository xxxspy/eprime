### 问卷指导语

负面网络口碑对消费者品牌转换意愿的影响

Dear Participant,
I am postgraduate student major in Advertising and Marketing at University of Hull. Currently I am looking for participants to take part in my study about the influence of negative  word-of-mouse on consumer switching intension of brand. This questionnaire is anonymous and the content of the survey will only be used as a research paper,we promise to keep all the data confidentially. The valuable information you provide is the key for this research,we sincerely appreciate your support.
Please read the contents of the questionnaire carefully and answer the questions according to your personal experience. Thank you for your support and participation!

Please think back to the negative information, comments and reviews you know about a certain product or brand through social Media or website (Weibo,Wechat,QQ,Facebook,Twitter,Insgram,YouTube,Snapchat,What's Up,etc),such as low safety factor of Japanese cars,Samsung mobile phone explosion,Apple leaks user's personal information and so on.Answer the following questions based on your impression of these information and comments.

Contact
Supervisee:Shaoting Lin S.Lin@2017.hull.ac.uk
Superviser:Stewart Arnold: s.arnold@hull.ac.uk

If you have any concerns about the conduct of this research, please contact the Secretary, HUBS Research Ethics Committee, University of Hull, Cottingham Road, Hull, HU6 7RX; Tel No. (+44) (0)1482 463410; Fax (+44) (0)1482 463689.


Glossary:
"Word-of-Mouse": “Word-of-Mouse” is a new version of the older term “word of mouth” that applies to the digital era – “Word-of-Mouse” is people passing along messages about a company or product (or anything else) through digital forums. (Techopedia,2018)

"Consumer Switching intension ": "Consumer Switching Intension" is define as a purchasing pattern that characterized by a change orswitch from one brand to another. (Peter and Olson,2000）

Original Concept: Our mission is to improve students’ confidence. The major focus of learning mate is on providing the highest quality tutoring about improving academic performance for high school students, then improving their confidence. The leaning mate is providing the professional tutor who can help students to improve their school grades and teach them how to learn efficiently and independently, then improving their confidence on academic study. The leaning mate can provide two ways for helping students to improve their grades, private tutor and lessons, either online or face to face. The price about improve grades for private tutor is $40 an hour, while the price of lessons is from $ 20- $35 per hour.

我们的使命是提高学生的自信心。 学习伴侣的主要目的是为提高高中生的学习成绩，提高他们的自信心提供最优质的辅导。 伙伴将为您提供专业的导师，他们可以帮助学生提高他们的学业成绩，教他们如何有效和独立地学习，从而提高他们对学术学习的信心。 学习伴侣可以通过两种方式（在线或面对面）提供帮助学生提高成绩的私人教练和课程。 私人教师提高成绩的价格是每小时40美元，而课程的价格是每小时15-30美元。

Our Concept: Our mission is to improve students’ confidence. The major focus of Color mind, color life is not only providing the highest quality tutoring about improving academic performance for high school students, but also on providing professional technology and experts to find what factors can affect students’ confidence, then make an individually plan for them to improve their confidence. Students can book consultation via either our online system or face to face, then our professional experts will provide a specific plan for students and assist them to complete their plan and improve their confidence. The price about improve grades for private tutor is $55 an hour, while the price of lessons is from $ 20- $35 per hour as same as previous. We will charge students $80 if students want to improve their confidence in other aspects.

我们的使命是改善学生因家庭离婚而产生的不信任感。 我们的主要重点是为提高中学生的学业成绩提供最高质量的辅导，而且还为解决家庭因素（离婚）带来的负面影响提供专业技术和专家支持。 为他们单独计划以提高他们的信心。 学生可以通过我们的在线系统或面对面的方式预订咨询，然后我们的专业专家将为学生提供具体的计划，帮助他们完成计划并提高他们的信心。 私人补习班成绩的价格为每小时40美元，而课程的价格与之前相同，为每小时15-30美元。 如果学生想通过私人教练提高自信心，我们将向学生收取55美元。

### 问卷指导语

亲爱的同学：
你好！我是一名海外教学院的学生，正在完成一篇关于来华菲律宾学生的论文，目的是希望得出菲律宾学生更好的在中国进行学习和生活的建议，所以想了解关于您作为一名留学生对中国现代文化的想法。
希望能希望您能百忙之中抽空完成这份问卷。
调查不需要提供您的名字，不记对错，希望您能真实回答以下问题。感谢您的支持与合作!
Dear friends:
Hello! I am a student of FuJian Normal University. I am completing a dissertation on Filipino students coming to China in order to get suggestions for Filipino students to study and live in China better. I want to know your thoughts on Chinese modern culture as an international student.
I sincerely hope you can spend time to complete the questionnaire.
For this survey, you are not required to provide your name. There are no right or wrong answers. I hope you can answer the following questions seriously. Thank you for your support and
cooperation!

(请根据实际情况填空。谢谢! Please fill in the blanks on the line, Thank you!)

该问卷主要用于统计学、计量经济学、行为经济学、教育学、心理学专业的样本收集。

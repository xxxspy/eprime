Hello Everyone!
I am an undergraduate student studying in global fashion management. I would like to invite you to participate in a survey on “The marketing activities influence consumers attitude and purchase intention on the fashion private brand of online retailers.” Before you accept to engage it, it is important that you understand what the research is and why it is being conducted.
大家好！
我是一名学习全球时尚管理的硕士生，我想邀请您参加我的毕业论文研究问卷。课题是营销策略是如何影响到消费者的态度和购买意愿对于网上零售商的时尚自有品牌。在答题之前我想简单的和您介绍一下我的研究话题。
Concept introduction 概念介绍
The online retailer refers to an intermediary that directly sells the products or services to consumers through the electronic form of transactions on the Internet platform. The private brand is a product that is exclusively manufactured for a retailer but manufactured by a third party. The retailer will sell the product under its own brand name. For example, the fashion brand “Inte Right”is part of JD.COM and the brand “ASOS EDITION” belong to fashion online retailer ASOS etc.
网上零售商是指通过互联网平台上的电子交易形式直接向消费者出售产品或服务的中介。 私人品牌是专为零售商制造但由第三方制造的产品。 零售商将以自己的品牌名称销售产品。 例如，品牌京东旗下的时尚品牌Inte Right 或者是英国的ASOS时尚网上零售商推出的ASOS特别版。等等

The questionnaire will take approximately 3 minutes to complete. Your participation in this research is entirely voluntary and your responses are completely anonymous. There are no right or wrong answers in this questionnaire, so please answer your truly personal perception. Finally, many thanks for your support in the research project.
问卷大约需要3分钟才能完成。 您参与本研究完全是自愿的，而且您的回答完全是匿名的。 此问卷中没有正确或错误的答案，因此请回答您的真实个人看法。 最后，非常感谢您对研究项目的支持。
If you have any questions about the survey, please let me know at sd19hz@leeds.ac

该问卷主要用于行为经济学、统计学、计量经济学、教育学、心理学专业的样本收集。

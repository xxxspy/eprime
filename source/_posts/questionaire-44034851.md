### 问卷指导语

Dear Mrs. / Mr.,
This is a questionnaire about the corporate image of Starbucks in the UK. The purpose is to understand your understanding and evaluation of the British Starbucks corporate image. Your opinion is very important to me and I hope to get your cooperation. The questionnaire is in an anonymous manner. Please tick the appropriate level according to your feelings. The content you filled in is for academic research only, and I promise not to disclose the privacy to others, so please feel free to fill it out. I do hope you will be able to spare a few minutes to reply to this request.
Thanks!
Yuting Peng

您好！
这是一份关于星巴克的企业形象的调查问卷。 目的是了解您对星巴克企业形象的理解和评价。 您的意见对我非常重要，我希望得到您的合作。 问卷以匿名方式进行。 请根据您的感受勾选相应级别。 您填写的内容仅用于学术研究，我保证不会向他人透露隐私，因此请随时填写。 我希望你能够花几分钟时间回复这个请求。
谢谢！

该问卷主要用于统计学、计量经济学、行为经济学、教育学、心理学专业的样本收集。

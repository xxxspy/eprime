<p>当各剂量组呈等比级数时，式(1)可简化为式(2);求半数致死量的标准误s<sub>lgLD</sub><sub>50</sub>可用式(3)。</p><p align="center"><img imgpath="/CRFDPIC/R200612059/R200612059.0203.4.png" align="absmiddle" src="/CRFDPIC/R200612059/R200612059.0203.4.gif"></p>
